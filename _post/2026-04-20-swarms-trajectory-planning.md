Research focused on avoiding deadlocks in high-density multi-agent environments. 

### Key Contributions
1. Development of a **Minimal-Communication** protocol.
2. Integration of Voronoi-based spatial partitioning.
3. Real-time obstacle avoidance in cluttered spaces.

Tested in Gazebo/ROS2 environments with up to 10 agents.
