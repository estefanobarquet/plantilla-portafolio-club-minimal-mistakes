This project implements a **YOLOv8** model to identify road hazards like potholes and cracks in real-time. 

### Technical Specifications
* **Hardware:** NVIDIA Jetson Nano, Logitech C920.
* **Software:** Python, OpenCV, PyTorch.
* **Accuracy:** 92% mAP on custom ESPOL dataset.

The system integrates geolocation data to map every detected issue on a dashboard.
